---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Frequently asked questions for merchants"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to know internal scoring criteria?">
    No. Operate based on app-visible status, liquidity requirements, and channel approval states.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run multiple payment channels?">
    Yes, each subject to its own approval and volume limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a user disputes my order?">
    Submit evidence promptly. The dispute resolves on-chain based on fault determination and the evidence provided.
    
    See the [Disputes](/merchants/disputes) page for detailed information on the dispute resolution process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause order assignments?">
    Yes. Toggle online/offline status or deactivate channels when you can't service orders.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Circle of Trust and how does it affect me?">
    A Circle is a community-backed group of merchants. Circle Admins oversee operations, and community members can delegate tokens to your Circle to earn yield. As a merchant, your primary interaction is through the merchant registry and payment channels that Circles wrap.
    
    See the [Circles of Trust](/merchants/circles-of-trust) page for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I earn revenue?">
    Merchants earn a share of transaction volume through the protocol's fee distribution model. You can withdraw accrued fees through the supported merchant operations flow. See [For Investors](/investors/start-here) for the full fee distribution breakdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where are the exact numeric thresholds?">
    Thresholds visible in your deployment/client are operational. Internal scoring criteria are not publicly documented.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
