## Becoming a Candidate

Anyone can become a Candidate by joining Discord. No XP requirement.

## Candidate to "Cipher" Requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Earn 500 XP">
    Accumulate minimum 500 XP through app usage and social activity. This demonstrates your engagement with the P2P ecosystem.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Attend Induction Session">
    Attend a mandatory induction session held on the 1st and 3rd Sunday of every month, conducted by your "Country Lead". These sessions cover:
    - The Liberation Network's mission and values
    - Role expectations and responsibilities
    - Communication protocols and tools
    - Community guidelines
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Open Roles">
    Check open roles posted by "Keymasters" in your track and country. Roles are posted in Discord with specific requirements and expectations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply via Discord">
    Submit your application through Discord ticket system. Include:
    - Your relevant experience
    - Why you want to join this specific track
    - Your availability and commitment level
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Interview with Keymaster">
    Pass an interview with the "Keymaster" for your chosen track. This is a conversation to assess fit, skills, and alignment with the mission.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Become a Cipher">
    Upon approval, receive your "Cipher" role with access to:
    - Track-specific channels
    - Weekly update channels
    - Resource libraries
    - Direct communication with your Keymaster
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The process is designed to ensure quality engagement while maintaining our anonymous-first principles. No identity verification is required at any stage.
</Info>
