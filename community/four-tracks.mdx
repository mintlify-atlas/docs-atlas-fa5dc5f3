All tracks operate at the country level, managed by "Country Leads". Each track has specific focus areas and success metrics.

## Track Overview

| Code Name | Focus | SLA Metric |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| **Relay Guild** | Discord/Telegram moderation, user support | Users/merchants onboarded |
| **Node Guild** | Colleges, local events, meetups | Users/merchants onboarded |
| **Signal Guild** | Social media content, regional accounts | Impressions |
| **Forge Guild** | Translations, docs, dev community | Commits/pages |

## Relay Guild

<Card title="Community Management" icon="comments">
  Focuses on Discord and Telegram moderation, providing user support, and onboarding new community members. Success is measured by users and merchants onboarded.
</Card>

## Node Guild

<Card title="Grassroots Engagement" icon="people-group">
  Drives local adoption through college programs, meetups, and community events. Success is measured by users and merchants onboarded through offline channels.
</Card>

## Signal Guild

<Card title="Social Media" icon="megaphone">
  Creates and manages regional social media content in local languages. Success is measured by impressions and engagement across platforms.
</Card>

## Forge Guild

<Card title="Technical Community" icon="code">
  Handles translations, documentation, and fostering local developer communities. Success is measured by commits and documentation pages contributed.
</Card>

<Note>
Each country has one Keymaster per track, creating a balanced structure that ensures local expertise and sustainable growth.
</Note>
