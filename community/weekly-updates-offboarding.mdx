## Weekly Update Requirements

All "Ciphers" and "Keymasters" must submit weekly updates to maintain accountability and coordination.

| Role | Reports To | Deadline |
|------|------------|----------|
| "Cipher" | "Keymaster" | Saturday 00:00 to Sunday 24:00 UTC |
| "Keymaster" | "Country Lead" | Saturday 00:00 to Sunday 24:00 UTC |
| "Country Lead" | "Core Team" | Saturday 00:00 to Sunday 24:00 UTC |

## What to Include in Updates

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Ciphers">
    - Activities completed this week
    - Key metrics (onboardings, impressions, commits, etc.)
    - Challenges encountered
    - Plans for next week
    - Support needed from Keymaster
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Keymasters">
    - Summary of team activities
    - Aggregate metrics across all Ciphers
    - Resource needs and blockers
    - Team performance highlights
    - Strategic initiatives for next week
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Country Leads">
    - Cross-track coordination updates
    - Country-level metrics and trends
    - Budget requests and utilization
    - Strategic opportunities and challenges
    - Inter-country collaboration needs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Auto-Offboarding Policy

<Warning>
**Missing 1 consecutive update:** Triggers a warning DM from your superior.

**Missing 2 consecutive updates:** Results in automatic role removal and move to @Alumni role.
</Warning>

Alumni can re-apply through the normal onboarding process at any time. This policy ensures active participation and helps maintain a healthy, engaged community.

<Info>
The weekly update system creates transparency, accountability, and helps identify issues early. It's a core part of how The Liberation Network operates efficiently across countries and tracks.
</Info>
