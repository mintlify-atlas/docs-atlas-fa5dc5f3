<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to reveal my identity to participate?">
    No. The program is anonymous-first at every level. You can contribute and earn compensation without revealing your real identity. This is a core principle of The Liberation Network—your work matters, not your identity.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is XP and how do I earn it?">
    XP is earned through app usage and social activity within the P2P ecosystem. You need 500 XP to be eligible for a "Cipher" role. XP demonstrates your engagement and understanding of the tools we're promoting.
    
    Ways to earn XP:
    - Using the P2P app for transactions
    - Engaging in community discussions
    - Participating in social activities
    - Contributing to community growth
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I miss a weekly update?">
    One missed update triggers a warning DM from your Keymaster or Country Lead. Two consecutive missed updates result in automatic role removal and move to the @Alumni role.
    
    This policy ensures:
    - Active participation from all contributors
    - Timely communication across the network
    - Fair distribution of compensation to active members
    
    Alumni can re-apply through the normal onboarding process at any time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I participate from any country?">
    You can join Discord from anywhere in the world. However, active tracks depend on which countries have appointed Country Leads.
    
    If your country doesn't have active tracks yet:
    - Join as a Candidate to learn and engage
    - Build your XP and community standing
    - Express interest in becoming a Country Lead
    - Help launch The Liberation Network in your region
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How are payments made?">
    All payments go directly to your P2P wallet. This demonstrates our commitment to using the financial sovereignty tools we promote.
    
    Payment schedule:
    - Monthly compensation for Ciphers and Keymasters
    - Monthly Spotlight awards
    - All payments in stablecoins via P2P protocol
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I switch tracks or work in multiple tracks?">
    Ciphers typically focus on one track to maintain quality and depth of contribution. However:
    - You can request a track change through your Country Lead
    - Exceptional contributors may be invited to support multiple tracks
    - Keymasters coordinate across tracks at the country level
    
    Discuss any interest in changing or expanding your role with your Keymaster.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is the time commitment for a Cipher role?">
    Time commitment varies by track and specific assignment, but generally:
    - Minimum: Weekly update submission and core responsibilities
    - Typical: 5-10 hours per week
    - Compensation reflects the scope of your assignment
    
    Discuss specific expectations with your Keymaster during the interview process.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How are Monthly Spotlight winners selected?">
    Three winners are selected each month by Country Leads and Core Team based on:
    - Exceptional impact or results
    - Innovation in approach or execution
    - Going above and beyond role expectations
    - Embodying Liberation Network principles
    
    Winners are announced in #hall-of-fame and receive $500 bonus.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I disagree with my Keymaster?">
    The Liberation Network has clear escalation paths:
    1. Discuss directly with your Keymaster first
    2. Escalate to your Country Lead if needed
    3. Contact Core Team for final resolution
    
    We value open communication and constructive feedback at all levels.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I become a Keymaster or Country Lead?">
    Yes! Progression is possible through demonstrated excellence:
    
    **To become a Keymaster:**
    - Excel as a Cipher in your track
    - Demonstrate leadership and mentorship abilities
    - Apply when positions open in your country/track
    
    **To become a Country Lead:**
    - Proven track record as a Keymaster
    - Cross-track coordination skills
    - Strategic thinking and community building expertise
    - Often involves launching The Liberation Network in a new country
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
