## Base Compensation

| Role | Monthly Salary |
|------|----------------|
| "Keymaster" | $200 |
| "Cipher" | Up to $100 (based on job assigned) |

<Info>
All payments are made directly to your P2P wallet, demonstrating our commitment to using the tools we promote.
</Info>

## Monthly Spotlight Awards

**$500 x 3 = $1,500 monthly prize pool**

Three outstanding contributors are selected each month for exceptional work, innovation, or impact. Winners receive an additional $500 bonus.

## Fund Approval Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keymaster Request">
    "Keymaster" raises funding request for their team's activities and compensation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Country Lead Review">
    "Country Lead" reviews the request for alignment with country goals and budget availability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Core Team Approval">
    "Core Team" provides final approval and budget allocation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Disbursement">
    Funds are disbursed directly to P2P wallets of approved recipients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Compensation is designed to be sustainable and fair while recognizing the varying levels of commitment and impact across different roles and assignments.
</Note>
