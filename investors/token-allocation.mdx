<Info>
No investor or team tokens unlock at TGE. At launch, 12.9M tokens circulate (10M sale + 2.9M liquidity).
</Info>

## Initial Circulating Supply

At Token Generation Event:
- **10,000,000 tokens** distributed to MetaDAO sale participants
- **2,900,000 tokens** provided to DEX liquidity pools
- **Total circulating:** 12,900,000 (50% of supply)

## Locked Allocations

**Team tokens (7.74M)** follow performance-based vesting tied to protocol milestones, not time. See [Vesting Schedules](/investors/vesting-schedules) for details.

**Investor tokens (5.16M)** have a 1-year cliff followed by quarterly unlocks over the next 12 months.
