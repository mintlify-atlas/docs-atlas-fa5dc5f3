<Warning>
The $P2P token provides governance rights and economic utility within the protocol, independently of any corporate entity. It carries no claim on foundation assets and no debt obligation. Value derives from protocol usage and network effects rather than any promise of returns.
</Warning>

## Token Nature

The $P2P token is:

- A **governance token** for protocol parameters and treasury decisions
- An **economic utility token** for staking and revenue participation
- **Not equity** in any company or foundation
- **Not a security** or investment contract
- **Not a debt instrument** with promised returns

## No Claims on Foundation Assets

Token holders have:

- **No ownership claim** on foundation corporate assets
- **No right to foundation profits** or balance sheet
- **No creditor status** in any bankruptcy or liquidation
- **Control over protocol resources** (IP, treasury, mint authority) via governance

## Value Derivation

Token value derives from:

- Protocol usage and transaction volume
- Network effects and ecosystem growth
- Governance-directed treasury deployment
- Staking yield and revenue participation

**Not from:**

- Promises of returns or profit sharing
- Foundation performance or assets
- Guaranteed buy-backs or price support
