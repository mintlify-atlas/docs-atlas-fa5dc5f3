## Supply Structure

The total supply at launch is fixed at **25.8 million tokens**. This represents the complete initial allocation across all categories.

At Token Generation Event (TGE), **12.9 million tokens** enter circulation:
- 10 million from the MetaDAO sale
- 2.9 million provided as DEX liquidity

This creates an initial float of **50% of total supply**.

## Future Token Issuance

<Warning>
Any future token minting must pass through MetaDAO futarchy governance, where market participants stake capital on whether proposals increase or decrease token value. Proposals predicted to harm value are automatically rejected.
</Warning>

The protocol is designed to run on transaction revenue, not token emissions.
