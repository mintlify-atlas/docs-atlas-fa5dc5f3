## What is P2P Protocol?

P2P Protocol reflects a departure from fiat escrows and traditional custodians by using **zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs** for non-custodial KYC, making on/off-ramps privacy-preserving and governed by users' collective interests.

In the wake of centralized exchanges, early decentralized exchanges led the DeFi movement using order books and liquidity pools. Yet DeFi users still lacked a fully decentralized way to onramp from fiat to crypto and offramp back to fiat.

<Note>
Currently deployed on the **Base network** with Solana deployment planned within six months.
</Note>

## How It Works

P2P Protocol operates through three core mechanisms:

1. **Trustless Matching** - Buyers are matched with highly vetted merchants according to a Proof-of-Credibility reputation algorithm
2. **On-Chain Settlement** - Trades are settled through on-chain coordination without platform custody
3. **Decentralized Dispute Resolution** - Disputes are resolved through on-chain admin settlement rather than centralized authority

## Key Concepts

### Zero-Knowledge Proofs

P2P Protocol uses zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs to enable non-custodial KYC. This means users can prove their credibility and identity without revealing sensitive personal information to a centralized entity, maintaining privacy while ensuring compliance.

### Proof-of-Credibility

The protocol implements a **Proof-of-Credibility reputation algorithm** that vets merchants and participants based on their on-chain behavior and transaction history. This creates a credibility-based DeFi ecosystem where:

- Peers can transact with confidence based on verifiable reputation
- Users build credibility over time through successful transactions
- The system doesn't rely on over-collateralized mechanics for everyday actions

<Warning>
Always verify merchant credibility scores and transaction history before engaging in high-value trades.
</Warning>

### Non-Custodial Trading

Unlike traditional centralized exchanges or platforms that hold user funds in escrow, P2P Protocol enables completely non-custodial trading:

- Users maintain control of their assets at all times
- No platform custody or escrow services
- Settlement happens directly on-chain between parties
- Disputes are resolved through transparent on-chain mechanisms

## The Vision

The end-state is a **credibility-based DeFi ecosystem** where peers transact, save, and build services on top of an open Proof-of-Credibility graph—useful, easy to use, privacy-first, and not reliant on over-collateralized mechanics for every everyday action.

This vision encompasses:

- A mature, protocol-neutral, global network
- Privacy-preserving financial infrastructure
- User-governed collective interests
- Open and composable credibility systems
