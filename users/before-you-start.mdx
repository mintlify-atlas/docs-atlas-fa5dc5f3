## What You Need

Before you begin trading on P2P Protocol, make sure you have:

- An account on a P2P Protocol client app (wallet is provided in-app via thirdweb integration)
- Access to supported payment rails in your region
- Stablecoin balance for SELL/PAY flows

## Basic Safety Checks

<Warning>
  Always follow these safety practices to protect your account and funds:
</Warning>

- **Verify the official domain** - Confirm you are on the official app domain before signing in
- **Double-check order details** - Verify order details (amount, currency, recipient) before submission
- **Protect your credentials** - Do not share your login credentials or account recovery information with anyone

<Tip>
  Bookmark the official app URL to avoid phishing attempts.
</Tip>
